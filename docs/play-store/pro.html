The heart of Pro

A real language model, running inside your keyboard.

Most AI keyboards ship your words to a server. Clicks Pro runs an actual LLM on your phone, in the keyboard process itself — so it works while typing into any app, with no account, no network, and nothing leaving the device.

Works where cloud AI can't — Android blocks system GenAI for background keyboards; an in-process model has no such limit, so it assists inside every app you type in.

Truly private — generation happens on your CPU. Your messages, drafts and context never touch a server — there's no server to touch.

Guaranteed-valid output — a grammar constrains the model as it writes, so structured results (like clean JSON for skills) can't come out malformed — stronger than cloud "JSON mode."

One download, then yours — the model fetches once on request (resumable), then loads lazily from disk. After that it's fully offline.

What the on-device AI does

It writes, rewrites and reacts — in the flow of typing.

Smart compose & rewrite — finish a thought, tighten a message, or shift its tone without leaving the field.

Clip-skills — type a trigger and it acts on your clipboard: translate, read the mood of a message, draft an xreply, and more.

Agentic results — rich answer cards appear right above the keys, with follow-up actions — never auto-sent, always your tap.

Same brain, both keyboards — the docked launcher keyboard and the system keyboard share one engine, so skills behave identically everywhere.
